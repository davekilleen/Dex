# Meal Planning

Weekly dinner planning for the Hadiari family. Regis does almost all the cooking.

## Household Rules

- **Prep for 5** — family of 4 but Sammy (15yo) eats for 2
- **Dinners only** — family is self-sufficient for breakfast and lunch
- **Cook big** — make 8-10 servings, leftovers cover at least one more dinner
- **3 big cooks per week** → 6 dinners covered, 1 night light/flexible

## Dietary Restrictions

**No:** dairy, peanut, tree nuts, eggs, avocado, banana, coconut milk
**Use instead:** oat milk (full fat/barista for cooking), olive oil, coconut cream (cans OK if tolerated — confirm)

## Kitchen Setup

- Stovetop and oven — no rice cooker, no Instant Pot
- Rice: 2 cups rice, 3 cups water, boil, lid on, low 18 min

## Grocery

- **When:** Weekends + Monday night (while Ella is at dance 8-9pm)
- **Where:** Whole Foods Coop (Duluth), Super One (Duluth), Costco
- **Costco:** bulk proteins, rice, pantry staples
- **Coop/Super One:** produce, specialty items, weekly fresh

## Meal Planning Rhythm

Plan dinners during `/week-plan` on Monday mornings:
1. Check Hadiari calendar for busy nights → leftovers or self-serve
2. Pick 3 big cook nights based on schedule openings
3. Generate grocery list split by store
