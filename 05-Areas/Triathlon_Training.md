## Training Week Structure

| Day | Focus | Why |
|-----|-------|-----|

## The Rules That Matter

1. **Consistency beats intensity.** Four easy sessions per week for 19 weeks.
2. **Down weeks are not optional.** Weeks 4, 7, and 13 are built-in recovery.
3. **The swim is the wild card.** Invest disproportionate time here.
4. **Nothing new on race day.** No new shoes, food, or gear.
5. **Listen to your body.** At 51, address issues early. A skipped session beats a skipped race.
