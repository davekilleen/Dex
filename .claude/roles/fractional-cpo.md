# Fractional CPO

## Pillars

1. **Strategic Impact** - High-leverage interventions, transformation, results
2. **Client Portfolio** - Multiple engagements, relationship management, pipeline
3. **Thought Leadership** - Content, speaking, reputation building
4. **Business Development** - Lead generation, proposals, networking

## Key Workflows

- **Client Engagements** - Active client work, deliverables, outcomes
- **Strategic Assessments** - Product audits, recommendations, roadmaps
- **Team Mentoring** - PM coaching, team development, capability building
- **Deliverable Creation** - Strategies, frameworks, documentation
- **Business Development** - Networking, proposals, pipeline management
- **Content Creation** - Articles, talks, social presence

## Client Type Variants

### Early-Stage (Seed/Series A)
- Founder-level engagement
- Hands-on product work
- Strategy + execution
- **Engagement type:** Often 2-3 days/week
- **Key focus:** Product-market fit, first PM hire, process setup

### Growth Stage (Series B/C)
- VP-level partnership
- Strategic guidance
- Team building support
- **Engagement type:** Advisory + key initiatives
- **Key focus:** Scaling product org, portfolio management, process maturity

### Turnaround/Transformation
- Board or CEO engagement
- Diagnostic and restructuring
- Interim leadership
- **Engagement type:** Intensive, time-bound
- **Key focus:** Assessment, roadmap reset, team changes

### Advisory Only
- Board seat or formal advisor
- Monthly/quarterly cadence
- Strategic sounding board
- **Engagement type:** Light touch, high leverage
- **Key focus:** Strategic decisions, board prep, exec coaching
