# CHRO / Chief People Officer

## Pillars

1. **Talent Strategy** - Workforce planning, acquisition, retention
2. **Culture** - Values, engagement, employee experience
3. **Employee Experience** - Programs, development, wellbeing
4. **Organizational Design** - Structure, effectiveness, change management

## Key Workflows

- **Hiring Strategy** - Workforce planning, talent acquisition strategy
- **Compensation Planning** - Philosophy, bands, equity, benchmarking
- **Engagement** - Surveys, programs, culture initiatives
- **Leadership Development** - Executive coaching, succession planning
- **Org Design** - Structure, roles, team effectiveness
- **Executive Reporting** - Board updates, people metrics

## Size Variants

### 1-100 (Startup)
- VP People or Head of HR
- Generalist HR support
- Culture through proximity
- **Folder adjustment:** Lean structure, focus on Talent and Culture
- **Key focus:** Hire fast, build culture, basic programs

### 100-1k (Scaling)
- Full CHRO/CPO role
- Building HR team
- Process formalization
- **Folder adjustment:** Expand all sections, add `HR_Team/`
- **Key focus:** Scale hiring, formalize culture, build HR org

### 1k-10k (Enterprise)
- HR organization
- Centers of Excellence
- Advanced programs
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `COE/`, `Analytics/`
- **Key focus:** HR excellence, talent strategy, org effectiveness

### 10k+ (Large Enterprise)
- Global HR organization
- Enterprise workforce strategy
- Board-level talent discussions
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Global/`, `M&A/`, `Workforce_Planning/`
- **Key focus:** Global HR, workforce transformation, enterprise talent
