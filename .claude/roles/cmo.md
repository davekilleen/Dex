# CMO

## Pillars

1. **Brand** - Positioning, identity, reputation, awareness
2. **Demand Generation** - Pipeline, leads, revenue marketing
3. **Market Position** - Competitive strategy, category leadership
4. **Customer Insights** - Market research, voice of customer, trends

## Key Workflows

- **Campaign Strategy** - Major campaigns, integrated marketing
- **Brand Management** - Positioning, messaging, visual identity
- **Market Research** - Competitive analysis, customer research, trends
- **Team Leadership** - Marketing org development, hiring
- **Budget Management** - Marketing spend, ROI optimization
- **Executive Reporting** - Board updates, metrics, attribution

## Templates

- `Campaign_Strategy.md` - Major campaign planning
- `Brand_Brief.md` - Brand positioning document
- `Market_Analysis.md` - Market research synthesis
- `Board_Update.md` - Marketing board presentation
- `Agency_Brief.md` - External agency briefing
- `Team_Review.md` - Marketing org assessment

## Size Variants

### 1-100 (Startup)
- VP Marketing or fractional CMO
- Hands-on execution
- Limited team
- **Folder adjustment:** Lean structure, focus on Campaigns and Brand
- **Key focus:** Brand foundation, demand gen, prove ROI

### 100-1k (Scaling)
- Full CMO role
- Building marketing org
- Channel expansion
- **Folder adjustment:** Expand Team, add `Channels/`
- **Key focus:** Scale marketing, team building, pipeline growth

### 1k-10k (Enterprise)
- Marketing organization
- Brand authority
- Global expansion
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Global/`, `Corporate_Marketing/`
- **Key focus:** Market leadership, brand building, global marketing

### 10k+ (Large Enterprise)
- Global marketing organization
- Corporate communications
- Industry influence
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Public_Relations/`, `Analyst_Relations/`
- **Key focus:** Market position, corporate brand, industry leadership
