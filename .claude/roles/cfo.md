## Pillars

1. **Financial Strategy** - Capital structure, funding, financial planning
2. **Capital Allocation** - Investment decisions, resource optimization
3. **Risk Management** - Financial risk, controls, compliance
4. **Investor Relations** - Fundraising, board reporting, stakeholder communication

## Key Workflows

- **Fundraising** - Rounds, debt, strategic planning
- **Financial Planning** - Budgeting, forecasting, scenario modeling
- **Board Reporting** - Financial updates, metrics, analysis
- **M&A** - Due diligence, valuation, integration
- **Treasury** - Cash management, banking, FX
- **Audit & Compliance** - External audit, SOX (if applicable), controls

## Size Variants

### 1-100 (Startup)
- VP Finance or fractional CFO
- Fundraising focus
- Lean finance team
- **Folder adjustment:** Focus on Fundraising and basic Operations
- **Key focus:** Runway, fundraising, financial foundation

### 100-1k (Scaling)
- Full CFO role
- Building finance team
- Process formalization
- **Folder adjustment:** Expand Operations, add `Team/`
- **Key focus:** Scaling finance, forecasting, series B/C

### 1k-10k (Enterprise)
- Finance organization
- Advanced planning
- Potential IPO prep
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `IPO_Readiness/`, `Compliance/`
- **Key focus:** Enterprise finance, IPO prep, strategic M&A

### 10k+ (Large Enterprise)
- Public company CFO
- Global finance
- Investor relations
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Public_Company/`, `Global/`, `IR/`
- **Key focus:** Shareholder value, global operations, capital markets
