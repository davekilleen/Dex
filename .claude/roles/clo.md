# CLO / General Counsel

## Pillars

1. **Legal Risk** - Risk identification, mitigation, management
2. **Compliance** - Regulatory, corporate, industry requirements
3. **Deal Support** - M&A, commercial agreements, strategic transactions
4. **Corporate Governance** - Board matters, entity management, fiduciary duties

## Key Workflows

- **Contract Review** - High-stakes negotiations, strategic agreements
- **Regulatory Compliance** - Regulatory tracking, policy development
- **IP Protection** - Patent strategy, trademark, trade secrets
- **Board Matters** - Governance, resolutions, fiduciary oversight
- **M&A** - Due diligence, transaction support, integration
- **Litigation** - Dispute management, claims, settlements

## Size Variants

### 1-100 (Startup)
- First GC hire or outside counsel
- Generalist legal support
- Deal and compliance focus
- **Folder adjustment:** Lean structure, focus on Corporate and Compliance
- **Key focus:** Enable deals, basic compliance, manage outside counsel

### 100-1k (Scaling)
- Full GC role
- Building legal team
- Process establishment
- **Folder adjustment:** Expand all sections, add `Legal_Ops/`
- **Key focus:** Build legal function, scale contract review, governance

### 1k-10k (Enterprise)
- Legal department
- Specialization
- Advanced governance
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Specialties/`, `International/`
- **Key focus:** Legal excellence, risk management, global compliance

### 10k+ (Large Enterprise)
- Global legal organization
- Multiple practice areas
- Board-level counsel
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Global/`, `Public_Company/`, `Government_Affairs/`
- **Key focus:** Enterprise legal, global operations, strategic counsel
