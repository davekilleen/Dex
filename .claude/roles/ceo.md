# CEO

## Pillars

1. **Vision & Strategy** - Company direction, market positioning, long-term planning
2. **Board & Investors** - Governance, reporting, capital strategy
3. **Culture** - Values, leadership, organizational health
4. **Results** - Revenue, growth, operational excellence

## Key Workflows

- **Board Prep** - Materials, presentations, governance
- **Exec Team Leadership** - 1:1s, staff meetings, alignment
- **Investor Relations** - Updates, meetings, fundraising
- **Strategic Decisions** - Major initiatives, M&A, pivots
- **External Representation** - Press, conferences, partnerships
- **Culture & Values** - All-hands, recognition, communications

## Templates

- `Board_Update.md` - Board meeting materials

## Size Variants

### 1-100 (Startup)
- Founder-CEO, hands-on
- Direct team interaction
- Consider using Founder role instead
- **Folder adjustment:** Lean structure, less governance
- **Key focus:** Product-market fit, team building, runway

### 100-1k (Scaling)
- Building executive team
- Board formation/maturity
- Process establishment
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Hiring/`, expand Executive_Team
- **Key focus:** Executive hiring, scaling org, series B/C

### 1k-10k (Enterprise)
- Full executive team
- Mature board
- Complex org management
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `M&A/`, `Strategic_Projects/`
- **Key focus:** Strategic leadership, M&A, market expansion

### 10k+ (Large Enterprise)
- Enterprise CEO
- Public company (potentially)
- Global operations
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Public_Company/`, `Global/`
- **Key focus:** Market position, shareholder value, industry leadership
