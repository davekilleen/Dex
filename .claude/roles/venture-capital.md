# Venture Capital / Private Equity

## Pillars

1. **Deal Flow** - Sourcing, screening, due diligence, investment decisions
2. **Portfolio Support** - Value creation, board work, founder guidance
3. **LP Relations** - Fundraising, reporting, investor communication
4. **Thesis Development** - Market research, sector expertise, investment strategy

## Key Workflows

- **Deal Sourcing** - Network mining, inbound screening, market mapping
- **Due Diligence** - Financial analysis, reference calls, technical assessment
- **Investment Committee** - Deal memos, recommendations, decision support
- **Board Participation** - Meeting prep, strategic guidance, governance
- **Portfolio Reviews** - Performance tracking, support needs, follow-on decisions
- **Fundraising** - LP meetings, data rooms, fund updates
- **Market Research** - Thesis development, competitive landscapes, sector deep dives

## Seniority Variants

### Analyst / Associate
Early career, supporting investment decisions:
- Heavy research and due diligence
- Market mapping and thesis support
- Deal sourcing and screening
- **Folder adjustment:** Emphasize `Thesis/` and `Deal_Pipeline/Sourcing/`
- **Key focus:** DD execution, market research, sourcing, deal memo drafting

### Principal / VP
Mid-level, driving deals with oversight:
- Deal ownership with partner supervision
- Some board observer seats
- Increasing sourcing responsibility
- **Folder adjustment:** Expand `Portfolio/` with board responsibilities
- **Key focus:** Deal execution, founder relationships, emerging board work

### Partner / GP
Senior, full investment authority:
- Board seats on portfolio companies
- LP relationships and fundraising
- Investment committee decisions
- Fund strategy
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Fund_Strategy/`, expand `LPs/`
- **Key focus:** Board work, LP relations, fund performance, strategic decisions

### Managing Partner / Founding Partner
Running the firm:
- Fund management and team leadership
- Primary LP relationships
- Firm strategy and culture
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Firm/` with team, operations, strategy
- **Key focus:** Firm building, LP fundraising, portfolio governance

## Stage Variants

### Early Stage (Seed/Series A)
- High volume sourcing
- Founder-centric diligence
- Product and market focus
- **Folder adjustment:** Emphasize `Thesis/` and founder relationships
- **Key focus:** Founder quality, market timing, product insight

### Growth Stage (Series B+)
- Financial-heavy diligence
- Unit economics focus
- Governance and scaling
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Financial_Models/`, emphasize `Portfolio/KPIs/`
- **Key focus:** Metrics, growth efficiency, market leadership

### Buyout / PE
- Control investments
- Value creation plans
- Operating partner involvement
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Operations/`, `Value_Creation/`
- **Key focus:** Operational improvement, exit planning, capital structure

## Fund Size Variants

### Emerging Manager (< $100M AUM)
- Lean operations, wearing many hats
- Heavy on sourcing and founder relationships
- LP cultivation critical
- **Folder adjustment:** Minimize structure, emphasize `LPs/Prospective_LPs/`
- **Key focus:** Reputation building, network development, LP fundraising

### Established Fund ($100M-$1B AUM)
- Defined roles and processes
- Regular portfolio cadence
- Institutional LP base
- **Folder adjustment:** Default structure works well
- **Key focus:** Process discipline, portfolio management, consistent returns

### Large Fund ($1B+ AUM)
- Platform services
- Multiple deal teams
- Complex LP relationships
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Platform/` for portfolio services
- **Key focus:** Platform value-add, multi-strategy coordination, brand leverage
