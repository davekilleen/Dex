# Consultant

## Pillars

1. **Client Delivery** - Project execution, quality, outcomes
2. **Methodology** - Frameworks, approaches, best practices
3. **Knowledge Building** - Research, expertise development, continuous learning
4. **Practice Development** - Reputation, business development, growth

## Key Workflows

- **Engagement Scoping** - Problem definition, approach, proposal
- **Research & Analysis** - Data gathering, synthesis, insights
- **Recommendations** - Findings, options, strategic advice
- **Stakeholder Management** - Client relationships, communication, alignment
- **Deliverable Creation** - Reports, presentations, documentation
- **Knowledge Capture** - Lessons learned, reusable assets, expertise building

## Templates

- `Engagement_Scope.md` - Project scope and approach
- `Research_Brief.md` - Research plan template
- `Analysis_Framework.md` - Analytical approach
- `Findings_Report.md` - Research findings format
- `Recommendation_Deck.md` - Strategic recommendations
- `Proposal.md` - Engagement proposal template

## Integrations

- **Calendar** - Client scheduling
- **Notion/Coda** - Project workspace

## Practice Area Variants

### Strategy Consulting
- C-suite engagement
- High-stakes decisions
- Broad business scope
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Strategy/`, emphasize stakeholder management
- **Key focus:** Strategic recommendations, executive influence, business impact

### Operations Consulting
- Process improvement
- Implementation focus
- Measurable outcomes
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Implementation/`, `Metrics/`
- **Key focus:** Process design, change management, operational excellence

### Technology Consulting
- Technical assessments
- Architecture recommendations
- Digital transformation
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Technical/`, `Architecture/`
- **Key focus:** Technology strategy, vendor selection, implementation planning

### Independent Consultant
- Solo practice
- Personal brand
- Business development
- **Folder adjustment:** Add `Business/`, emphasize Pipeline and Content
- **Key focus:** Client relationships, thought leadership, practice growth
